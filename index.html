Scum - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

Scum is a card game that uses Apple's GameKit and CloudKit services. We respect your privacy and collect minimal data necessary for the game to function.

Data We Collect

Game Center Data: When you sign in with Game Center, Apple provides your Game Center display name and player ID to enable multiplayer features. This is handled entirely by Apple's GameKit framework.

CloudKit Data: When you create or join a public game room, your Game Center display name and a room code are stored temporarily in Apple's CloudKit service to allow other players to find and join your game. This data is automatically deleted when the room expires.

Game Statistics: Your game scores, win/loss records, and player profile are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your real name, email address, phone number, or location. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Apple Game Center and Apple CloudKit. These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Scum is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All multiplayer communication is limited to predefined emoji reactions.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
